What is the fixed assets to net worth?
the fixed assets to net worth is a measurement of the solvency of the company.It is also an indicator for the proportion of the company's fixed assets which can't be used for meeting it's debt obligations.
How to calculate fixed assets to net worth?
To calculate fixed assets to net worth you should use the following formula:
fixed assets to net worth = net fixed assets / tangible net worth
Why use the fixed assets to net worth?
Investors usually use the fixed assets to net worth to measure the ability of a company to pay off it's long term debt.
